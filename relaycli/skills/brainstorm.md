name: brainstorm
description: Understand intent and scope before building (after obra/superpowers)
---
Understand what is actually wanted before writing code.

- Restate the request in one sentence: who needs what, and why now.
- List what is ambiguous. If the user is present, ask the one or two
  questions that change the design most; otherwise state your assumptions
  explicitly at the top of your reply and proceed.
- Pick the smallest scope that genuinely satisfies the request; name what
  you are deliberately NOT doing.
- For anything non-trivial, present a short numbered plan (files touched,
  verification step) before making large changes.
- Do not gold-plate: extra features nobody asked for are scope creep, not
  generosity.
